Instructions


  1. Visit the SIG's page for which you wish to apply. Click on the APPLY FOR RECRUITMENT button to view the details of that SIG's recruitment process.

  2. Click on the Register button on that page to be redirected to the registration form.

  3. You must register separately for every SIG you want to apply for.

  4. You will be notified via email and WhatsApp about any recruitment round timing and required information. Make sure you check these frequently.

  5. We will be having video interviews on Google Meet, the links of which will be sent to you after the online tests.

  6. If you have any issues, contact the POC for that SIG directly.
