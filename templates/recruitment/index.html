Instructions


  1. Visit the SIG's page for which you wish to apply (see Navbar). Click on the APPLY FOR RECRUITMENT button on that page to view the details of that SIG's recruitment process.

  2. Click on the Register button on that page to be redirected to the registration form.

  3. You must register separately for every SIG you want to apply for.

  4. You will be notified via email and WhatsApp about any recruitment round timing and required information. Make sure you check these frequently.

  5. We will be having video interviews on Microsoft Teams, the details of which will be sent to you after the online tests.

  6. If you have any issues, contact the POC for that SIG directly.


FAQs


  1. Can I apply for multiple SIGs?
    Yes. You can apply for as many SIGs as you want to. Fill out the form for each SIG you want to apply for separately.

  2. How will I know if I am selected for the next round?
    You will receive an email and a message on WhatsApp regarding your selection for each round.

  3. Where can I view the details of the recruitment process for a SIG?
    Click on the APPLY FOR RECRUITMENT button on the SIG's page to view the details of that SIG's recruitment process.

  4. Does ISTE recruit 3rd years?
    Yes we do! We do consider exceptional 3rd year candidates as well, however a majority of our intake will comprise of 2nd years.
